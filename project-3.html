Project Overview

A web app that solves Sudoku puzzles in seconds. You can enter your own puzzle, or generate a random one. You can check if your input is valid, or let the app solve it for you using a smart algorithm. You can also see the steps of the solution, and learn how to improve your Sudoku skills.
